Gameplay


  • The game is an action role-play so the purchasing of in-game items like cybernetic enchancemts, cars and guns are availabe to your liking.
  • To maximize your immersion, the game has no loading screens, is open world and in the first person.
  • You can fully customize you character voice, gender, skin color and natural skills.
  • You can customize your character to prioritize aggressive approaches and brutal force at the beginning of the game. This style is called 'Strong Solo' which enhances certain abilities at the start. You can choose 'NetRunner' style that takes a conservative approach to their journey.
  • You can roleplay as a 'Nomad', a person who roams the scrapyard lookings for tech and grew up in a clan learning freedom, interity and honesty. 'Streetkid', a person who was raised by outlaws. You can also choose 'Corporate', a no-nonsense type character who has no regard for human life.
  • Everything is connected, in-game items will interact with you according to you in-game choices, including character skills and story endings.
  • All your choices affect your reputation and will affect how non-playable characters intereact with you, which may even determine all or foe.

My Opinion
