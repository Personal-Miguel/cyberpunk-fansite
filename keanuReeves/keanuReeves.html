Keanu Reeves

    Keanu Reeves surprised everyone when he made an unexpected appearance in the trailer and at the E3 expo in the summer of 2019. If you watch the video linked, you can see both fans and Keanu embraced each other with love. Read below to learn more and his role in Cyberpunk 2077.







About Keanu

    Born on September 2, 2019. Keanu Reeves is of Chinese, Hawaiian and European descent. His name translates to 'Cool Breeze Over the Mountains'. Keanu Reeves is a well-respected actor in Hollywood. He is most known for his roles in the series 'The Matrix', 'Speed', and 'John Wick'. He has rightfully earned 'Best On-Screen Duo' with Sandra Bullock in Speed, 'Best Male Performance, Favourite Actor and Fight Scene' in 'The Matrix'. He also has the reputation of being genorous giving to causes well-known or not.

Johnny Silverwhat?

     No, you can't be Keanu in-game sadly, but you can be friends with him! Keanu Reeves will play as digital hallucination of the main character. Johnny Silverhand is a musical artist in a band called 'Samurai'. He's a rebel of the military who protests through music. When Keanu Reeves was approached he accepted the role and was ask for his input in how the role should be implemented. More to come...
